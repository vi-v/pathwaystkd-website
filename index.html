    Making a difference

    Pathways Tae Kwon-Do is affiliated with Pathways for Achievement, a not-for-profit organization aimed at promoting the individual success and citizenship of at-risk teenagers. The value structure upon which Pathways is based holds that all people have dignity and infinite worth. Disciplined martial arts practices along with community service activities are the basis for this exciting program.
